Diminishing Returns — CAC Optimiser

Define spend/CPE curves for each program and channel, then let your manager allocate budget and instantly see the impact on CAC. Based on historical conversion rates from 55-week cohort data.

Step 1 — Define diminishing returns curves

For each program, enter your expected CPE at three spend levels. The calculator fits a curve through these points and uses it to price any spend amount. Defaults are pre-populated from 55-week historical averages with ±40% elasticity.

Step 2 — Manager allocation view

Pick a spend level for each program × channel using the presets or type a custom amount. CPE and enrollments update automatically from your curves above. The goal is to find the combination that minimises overall CAC.
